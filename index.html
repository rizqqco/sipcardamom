Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for private events and 6–8 weeks for weddings. That said, reach out even if your event is coming up soon — we'll do our best to accommodate.

Is there a minimum booking?

Our minimum booking is 2 hours. There's no minimum guest count — we're happy to serve intimate gatherings of 10 or celebrations of 300+.

Do you need power or water?

We need access to a standard 120V electrical outlet. We bring our own water supply, so you don't need to worry about plumbing.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 25% deposit to hold your date, with the remaining balance due on the day of your event.

How much space does the cart need?

Our cart requires approximately a 6×8 ft footprint. We can set up both indoors and outdoors.

Do you travel outside NJ & NY?

Our primary service area is New Jersey and New York. For events further away, reach out and we'll let you know if we can make it work — we love a good road trip.
