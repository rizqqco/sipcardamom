Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 2–4 weeks for private events and 6–8 weeks for weddings. That said, reach out even if your date is soon — we'll do our best to accommodate.

Is there a minimum booking requirement?

Our minimum booking is 2 hours. There's no minimum guest count — we're happy to serve intimate gatherings of 10 or celebrations of 300+.

Do you need power or water on-site?

We need access to a standard 120V electrical outlet. We bring our own water supply — no plumbing required on your end.

How much space does the cart need?

Our cart requires approximately a 6×8 ft footprint and works both indoors and outdoors. We handle the setup and breakdown entirely.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — a 25% deposit holds your date, with the remainder due on the day of your event.

Do you travel outside NJ & NY?

Our primary area is New Jersey and New York. For events further out, reach out and we'll let you know if we can make it work — we're open to it.
